  <h2>1. Project Title and Overview:</h2>
  <p><strong>Project Title:</strong> FreelanceHub App Development</p>
  <p><strong>Overview:</strong> This project aims to create a comprehensive freelancing platform that connects clients with skilled freelancers for various services.</p>

  <h2>2. Project Objectives:</h2>
  <ul>
    <li>Provide a user-friendly platform for clients to post projects and hire freelancers.</li>
    <li>Enable freelancers to showcase their skills, bid on projects, and collaborate with clients.</li>
    <li>Implement secure payment processing for project transactions.</li>
  </ul>

    <li>Payment gateway integration</li>
    <li>User profiles and rating system</li>
  </ul>
